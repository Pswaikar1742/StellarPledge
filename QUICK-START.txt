┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 🎯 STEP 3: Demo Flow (5 minutes)                                │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

A. CREATE A CAMPAIGN (as Creator)
   ─────────────────────────────────
   1. Connect wallet with CREATOR secret key
   2. Create Campaign:
      • Goal: 100 XLM
      • Deadline: 1 hour
   3. Click "Create Campaign"
   4. Wait ~5 seconds for confirmation

B. PLEDGE TO CAMPAIGN (as Backer)
   ──────────────────────────────────
   1. Disconnect wallet
   2. Connect with BACKER secret key
   3. Click "Refresh Campaigns"
   4. Click on the campaign
   5. Enter pledge: 60 XLM
   6. Click "Pledge Now"
   7. See progress bar at 60%

C. COMPLETE CAMPAIGN (as Backer)
   ──────────────────────────────────
   1. Pledge another 40 XLM
   2. Campaign reaches 100%
   3. Status changes to "Successful"

D. CLAIM FUNDS (as Creator)
   ──────────────────────────────────
   1. Disconnect and reconnect as CREATOR
   2. Refresh campaigns
   3. Click on the successful campaign
   4. Click "Claim Funds"
   5. Check wallet balance - increased by 100 XLM!

E. DEMO REFUND (Optional)
   ──────────────────────────────────
   1. As creator, create campaign with 0.02 hour deadline
   2. As backer, pledge 50 XLM (goal is 200)
   3. Wait ~1 minute for deadline
   4. Status changes to "Failed"
   5. Click "Withdraw Refund"
   6. Get money back!

Solution: ✓ Check secret starts with 'S'
